# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the **TranspaGovChain FAQ** — here you’ll find answers to common questions about the platform, blockchain integration, and governance.

---

### ❓ What is TranspaGovChain?

TranspaGovChain is a **blockchain-powered transparency platform** built on **Polkadot**.  
It enables the government, citizens, and auditors to track public infrastructure projects in real-time with full accountability.

---

### ❓ How does it ensure transparency?

Every transaction — from project creation to completion — is recorded on an **immutable blockchain ledger**.  
This prevents tampering, fraud, or hidden changes.

---

### ❓ What is the TGC Token?

The **TGC Token** is the platform’s native cryptocurrency.  
It is used for:
- Governance voting  
- Staking rewards  
- Accessing advanced API features  
- Contractor reputation scoring  

---

### ❓ Is my personal data safe?

Yes. TranspaGovChain uses:
- **End-to-end encryption**  
- **Polkadot DID (Decentralized Identity)**  
- **Zero-Knowledge Proofs** for private data validation  

No private or sensitive information is ever publicly visible.

---

### ❓ Can citizens interact with the blockchain?

Absolutely!  
Citizens can use the **Transparency Dashboard** to:
- View project details  
- Verify blockchain records  
- Submit feedback or reports  

No blockchain experience is required — everything is user-friendly.

---

### ❓ How is the project governed?

Governance is handled by a **DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization)**.  
TGC token holders can vote on:
- Platform updates  
- Budget allocations  
- Feature proposals  
- Community-driven improvements
